
JavaScript Operators
--------------------
-> Assignment Operator: =
-> Arithmetic Operator: + - / * %
-> Short hand Math Operator: += , -= , /= , *= 
-> Conditional Operator: < , > , <= , >=, !==
-> Unary Operator: ++ , --
-> Logical Operator: && , || , ^
-> Ternary Operator: ?:
-> Equality Operator: == , ===
